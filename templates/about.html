About Me

This is what I do.

Hey there!

My name is Alena Harmony Adabase, but here, you can call me Dee . Why Dee? Well, maybe one day, I’ll divulge that secret. This space is where I share stories—woven from real-life experiences, intertwined with scripture, poetry, and short fiction.

Writing has always been my way of expressing myself and making sense of the world, and I hope my words resonate with you. Let’s explore life together—one story, one verse, one reflection at a time.

Welcome to Tea from Dee.
